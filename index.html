CringeIn Privacy Policy

Effective Date: 29th June 2025

Overview

CringeIn is a browser extension that filters LinkedIn content using AI analysis. We are committed to protecting your privacy and being transparent about our data practices.

Data We Collect

OpenAI API Key

You provide your OpenAI API key, which is stored locally in your browser and used only to authenticate with OpenAI's content analysis service.

LinkedIn Content

The extension reads LinkedIn post content from your feed to perform AI-powered analysis. This content is:

  • Sent to OpenAI's API for cringe detection analysis
  • Temporarily cached locally in your browser for performance
  • Never stored permanently or shared with other parties

Settings and Preferences

Your filter preferences, sensitivity settings, and usage statistics are stored locally in your browser.

Data We Don't Collect

We do not collect, store, or have access to:

  • Personal identification information
  • Email addresses or contact information
  • Financial or payment information
  • Location data
  • Browsing history outside of LinkedIn content analysis

Third-Party Services

The extension uses OpenAI's API to analyze LinkedIn content. Please review OpenAI's Privacy Policy for information about their data handling practices.

Data Security

All data is stored locally in your browser using secure storage mechanisms. Your API key and preferences are never transmitted to our servers.

Data Retention

All data is stored locally on your device. You can clear this data at any time by uninstalling the extension or using the extension's clear cache feature.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify users of any significant changes through the Chrome Web Store listing.
